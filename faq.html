FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare a manuscript for submission?

All instructions for preparing the manuscript for SJEAA can be found in our journal website under section of Author Guidelines.

How do I submit my manuscript?

Please submit your manuscript online through ScholarOne.

What happens after my manuscript has been accepted?

The manuscript files will be dealt with by the editorial and production team. Each manuscript will undergo editing process, and you, being the corresponding author will be contacted through email. You may be asked to answer the comments on the manuscript or provide new images and any delay to responding to these comments is likely to result in a delay in publishing your manuscript. Once the article has been assigned to a journal issue, you will be sent a proof for approval.

How can I find out the status of my manuscript?

Authors can check the status of submitted manuscript by logging into ScholarOne.

How long does it take to receive a decision?

First decision on any manuscript will be provided within three months of submission.

How much does it cost to publish in the SJEAA?

SJEAA does NOT charge any fee to authors.

How can I become a reviewer for the SJEAA?

You can email (sjeaa@ncaaa.org.sa) your interest with information about your research interests and specialties.

What exactly is EarlyCite?

SJEAA is pleased to offer EarlyCite to its authors and readers. Articles appearing in this section have been peer-reviewed and accepted for publication in this journal and posted online before print publication. These articles can be cited and quoted using the DOI as the reference source.

I am an invited author. How do I submit my manuscript when I have completed writing it?

All authors should submit their manuscript through ScholarOne Manuscripts. Please click Manuscript Submission and follow the instructions. You will need to create an account if you do not register already.
