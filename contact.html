Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical website design project takes 4-8 weeks, while larger products may take 3-6 months.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable working across different time zones and communication preferences.

What is your design process?

I follow a user-centered design process: Research → Strategy → Design → Testing → Delivery, with regular client collaboration throughout.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I provide support packages and retainer options for ongoing design needs, updates, and improvements.

Can you help with development as well?

While I focus on design, I have trusted development partners I can recommend and collaborate with for implementation.

What tools do you use?

I primarily use Figma for design, with additional tools like Miro for workshops, Notion for documentation, and various prototyping tools.
