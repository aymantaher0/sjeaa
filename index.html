Saudi Journal for Evaluation and Academic Accreditation (SJEAA)

is the official journal of the National Center for Academic Accreditation and EvAluation (NCAAA), Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA). It is published by Emerald Publishing, on behalf of NCAAA.

SJEAA publishes original peer-reviewed research articles, reviews, systematic reviews, conceptual papers, policy-related findings and best practices in the field of related to the quality in higher education, academic accreditation and ranking at the national, regional and international levels.

SJEAA serves as a medium for academicians, researchers, educational consultants, administrators, students, educational quality supervisors, academic reviewers, scholars, program assessors and policy makers from higher education institutions (HEIs), accrediting accreditation agencies and professional associations worldwide, to share their knowledge, research and good practices that would aid private and public HEIs to qualify for institutional and program accreditations of various levels and types.

SJEAA publishes 2 issues a year.

ADVERTISE WITH US

Greetings from NCAAA!

We invite you to send contributions/news reports/announcements in relation to Quality in Higher Education and Academic Accreditation for the publishing in our popular NCAAA News under the following categories:

  • • Recent Attainment
  • • Evaluation
  • • Job opportunities
  • • Good practices

Recent Attainment
Stories on your recent attainment in academic accreditation can be shared with word counts of 150-500. Images used must be high resolution.

Evaluation
One can share various key performance indicators (KPIs) and their evaluation methods in quality in higher education. This would aid policy makers to enrich their knowledge while moving towards academic accreditation.

Job opportunities
Universities can share their job vacancies to the academics around the world. This assists them to familiarize their brand to the extensive audience.

Good Practices in Higher Education Universities can share their good practices to the academics around the world

Please reach out to sjeaa@ncaaa.org.sa for further information.
