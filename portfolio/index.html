Process & Thinking

How I approach design challenges

My process is rooted in understanding context before solutions. Every product challenge is unique, but my framework remains consistent: clarity, collaboration, and continuous iteration.

01

Discovery & Research

Begin by understanding the problem space deeply. User interviews, competitive analysis, stakeholder alignment, and data review form the foundation for informed decisions.

02

Define & Strategize

Synthesize insights into clear problem statements and strategic direction. Define success metrics, prioritize opportunities, and align cross-functional partners.

03

Ideate & Prototype

Explore multiple solutions through sketching, wireframing, and rapid prototyping. Test assumptions early and often with users and stakeholders.

04

Design & Refine

Create high-fidelity designs with attention to interaction details, accessibility, and system consistency. Collaborate closely with engineering throughout.

05

Ship & Learn

Launch deliberately, measure impact against defined metrics, gather feedback, and iterate. Great products are never truly finished.
