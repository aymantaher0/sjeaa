About Me

Create an excellent User Experience for your users on the web and mobile platforms.

Ayman Taher, the Product Design Lead at Aman E-Payment, is the founder and CEO of Syntax, a Creative Education Company in the MENA region based in Egypt.

With over a decade of experience in product design and user experience, I've had the privilege of working with startups, enterprises, and educational institutions to create meaningful digital experiences.

My passion lies in bridging the gap between design excellence and education, empowering the next generation of designers with the skills and mindset needed to thrive in the digital age.
