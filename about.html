About
THE JOURNAL

SJEAA is owned by National Center for Academic Accreditation and EvAluation (NCAAA) and is published by Emerald Publishing under a platinum open access arrangement, in that all charges for publishing an open access article in this journal are funded by NCAAA.

Vision

To share and thrive science, knowledge and academic researches

Mission

To support scientific and academic publishing in the field of quality and academic accreditation

Aim

Short term goals

  • To address the needs of stakeholders in higher education for information on the most recent trends and findings in quality and academic accreditation

Short term goals

  •  To share science, knowledge & academic researches
  •  To encourage scientific papers in the field of quality and academic accreditation
  •  To deliver adequate knowledge on quality and academic accreditation & disseminate good practices across higher institutions.
  •  To streamline the peer-review process using sufficient strength of potential reviewers and editorial board members

Long term goals

  • To deliver the current issues and possible strategies available in quality and academic accreditation
  • To aid the stakeholders of tertiary education to reveal the importance of quality and academic accreditation
  • To strengthen institutional research and evaluation processes by providing a standard platform for academicians, researchers, and administrators to share their research on quality and accreditation in higher education
  • To improve and sustain the quality of publishing process by adhering to international standards
  • To showcase best practices adopted by various educational institutions by adhering to the bounds of quality and academic accreditation.

All contributors who do not meet the criteria for authorship should be listed in an acknowledgements section at the end of the manuscript.

Open Access – No Author Fee

SJEAA is owned by NCAAA and is published by Emerald Publishing under a platinum open access arrangement, in that all charges for publishing an open access article in this journal are funded by NCAAA. There is no charge to the author.


All articles in SJEAA are published as open access under a Creative Commons CC-BY license 4.0 (CC BY 4.0). For further information on licensing, please see the author guidelines. You can view the journal's transparency statement here.

Peer Review

SJEAA operates a double-blind peer review model. All articles undergo an initial assessment by the journal editor. If they are considered suitable for peer review, articles will then be a reviewed by a minimum of two external reviewers to assess its suitability for publication. Final responsibility for editorial decisions rest with the journal editor.

About NCAAA

NCAAA, the owner of SJEAA, is the authority responsible for academic accreditation and quality assurance in public and private HEIs in KSA.

This independent legal entity with administrative and financial governance is supervised by the Education Evaluation Commission (EEC), the competent authority in the Kingdom to assess and accredit education and training institutions and programs, in order to raise their quality, adequacy and contribution to the national economy and development.

Visit www.ncaaa.org.sa for more details about NCAAA.
